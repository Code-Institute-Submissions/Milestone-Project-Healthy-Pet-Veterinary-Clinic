Services

Friendly and Professional Veterinary Care

At Healthy Pet Veterinary Clinic provides veterinary services for every stage in your pet's life, from their first shots to a lifetime of preventive care. We invite you to explore our website to learn more about our services and facilities we provide to help keep your pet happy and healthy.

Our Services


Our comprehensive range of services for small animals includes the following:

  • Vaccinations
  • Surgical procedures including routine neutering, cryosurgery and specialist orthopaedic and soft tissue surgery all carried out in dedicated operating theatre where each patient is assessed prior to undergoing surgery and anaesthetic agents chosen accordingly.
  • Cardiology including long term management and supportive care.
  • Dentistry (advanced techniques are also available)
  • Micro-chipping
  • Dietary advice
  • Specialist Prescription Food
  • Health profiles including geriatric profiles
  • Fertility management
  • Pet Passports
  • Senior Pet Care

Our Facilities


Our modern, purpose-built practice is fully equipped with state-of-the-art facilities includes the following.

  • MRI
  • CT Scan
  • Hematology and biochemistry blood test analyser
  • Serology and hormone assays
  • Microscopy / Skin scrapes, hair plucks, blood and vaginal smears
  • Endocrinology
  • Ultrasound
  • Blood Pressure Measurement
  • Arthroscopy
  • Cryosurgery
  • Oncology
  • Cytology
  • X-ray including myelogram
  • ECG
  • Endoscopy
